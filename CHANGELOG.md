## [Sprint 2] — 2026-05-23

### Changed (breaking)

- Per-class tagged-PDF retrofit: all 11 `pub-*.cls` classes now emit
  PDF/A-4f tagged output under the `[tagged]` class option. Authors

  see `docs/tagged-pdf.md`.
